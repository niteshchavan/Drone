Transmitter — Circuit Connections (ESP32)

Components:
- ESP32 development board 
- 2x analog joysticks (each has VCC, GND, VRx, VRy)
- 1x toggle switch 

Wiring (matches src/tx.cpp pin constants):
- JOY1 (Throttle / Yaw)
  - VRx (throttle)  -> GPIO 34  (JOY1_X_PIN)
  - VRy (yaw)       -> GPIO 35  (JOY1_Y_PIN)
  - VCC             -> 3.3V
  - GND             -> GND

- JOY2 (Roll / Pitch)
  - VRx (roll)      -> GPIO 33  (JOY2_X_PIN)
  - VRy (pitch)     -> GPIO 32  (JOY2_Y_PIN)
  - VCC             -> 3.3V
  - GND             -> GND

- Toggle Switch (Channel 5/aux1)
  - One terminal     -> GPIO 25  (SWITCH_PIN)
  - Other terminal   -> GND
